Ketogenic (Keto) Diet

High-fat, low-carbohydrate diet. Aims to induce ketosis, where the body burns fat for energy instead of carbohydrates. Includes meats, fatty fish, eggs, dairy, oils, and low-carb vegetables.

Mediterranean Diet

Emphasizes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, nuts, and legumes. Includes moderate consumption of fish and poultry. Olive oil is the primary fat source. Reduces red meat and sweets intake.
